Since ancient times, there exists four realms that harbor all life forms serving as pillars supporting our universe

As time went by, its resident occupants prospered and congregate at the center of the universe forming a single collective super nation

Prospered as they were, envy and greed, the devil incarnate itself, eventually find its way corrupting pure minds, gouging out gentle minds, plaguing any decency left.
Thus, deserting their sanctuaries, separating into three technocratic war councils and waging war at each other was the only option left for them

Countless battles, endless deaths afford no more consolation. Exhausted and wasted they finally make peace. Devastated homes forced them to join hands once more in their true homeland. 
Forming circular border as they go about their newly-found life as to ward away any sense of hostility
